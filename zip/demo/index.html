About Osafi Inn

Nestled in the heart of Meghalaya's breathtaking landscapes, Osafi Inn offers an authentic and luxurious homestay experience that captures the essence of this magical region.

Our family-run guest house combines traditional Khasi architecture with modern amenities, providing guests with a unique opportunity to experience local culture while enjoying the comfort of a premium accommodation. From the moment you arrive, you'll be greeted by warm hospitality and stunning views of rolling hills, cascading waterfalls, and lush green valleys.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by the Khasi family, Osafi Inn was born from a passion to share the beauty and culture of Meghalaya with travelers from around the world. What started as a small family home has grown into a beloved destination for those seeking authentic experiences in one of India's most beautiful states.

Local Attractions

Must Visit

Cherrapunji (Sohra)

Famous for its living root bridges and spectacular waterfalls, Cherrapunji is one of the wettest places on Earth. Experience the majestic Nohkalikai Falls and the unique double-decker living root bridge.

2 hours drive
Full day tour
₹2,500 per person
Living Root Bridges Nohkalikai Falls Seven Sisters Falls
Capital City

Shillong

The capital city known as the "Scotland of the East" with its rolling hills, pleasant climate, and vibrant culture. Explore local markets, museums, and enjoy the city's musical heritage.
